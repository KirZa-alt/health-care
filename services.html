Home Healthcare Services provide high quality affordable medical services within the comfort of your home. Our services also aim to provide convenience to those patients who find it difficult to travel to health care centres to avail these services. Our highly skilled and experienced team of Doctors, Nurses and Physiotherapists provide care with compassion at your doorstep. This service is specifically designed to meet the health care needs of elderly people, people with chronic illnesses and disabilities, who are home bound, patients recovering from surgery or post discharge patients having special health care needs.

Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Services

Physiotherapy is a health care profession primarily concerned with the correction of impairments and disabilities. Physiotherapy focuses on promotion of mobility, functional ability, quality of life and movement potential .This is achieved through examination, evaluation diagnosis and physical intervention.

The Department of Physiotherapy at Liaquat National Hospital provides excellence in clinical care and education in the field of rehabilitation. Aiming for an optimal quality of life for individuals with physical disabilities.

Services and Treatment

The department provides extensive inpatient and outpatient clinical services.
These includes:

  • Pain management
  • Neuro and Spinal Rehabilitation
  • Hydrotherapy
  • Women Health
  • Pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Intensive and Respiratory Care

Caring for the elderly because they deserve the best!


National Hospital pioneers in establishing a one of its kind facility for elderly in any healthcare set up in Pakistan. The Senior Citizen Primary Care Unit (SCPCU), a state-of-the-art facility was inaugurated in October 2019.

With an increasing elderly population in our country, SCPCU strives to provide seniors with comprehensive health and daycare services in their golden years.

Many families struggle to care for their elderly family members at home. Loneliness and boredom could be a major issue in their lives, which can lead to depression and other health problems. That's where the Senior Citizens Primary Care Unit comes in. We provide a safe and secure environment where seniors can get an engaging social life and the healthcare attention they need.

Our team consists of highly qualified professionals, including doctors, nurses, psychologists, physiotherapists, occupational therapists, and dietitians, to provide comprehensive and compassionate care.

SCPCU goes above and beyond to meet the needs of its elderly members. The compassionate staff members are dedicated to providing personalized care, grooming assistance, mobility facilitation, and maintaining proper hygiene.

Whether for a few hours or the entire day, our members can stay in a friendly environment, addressing their medical, social, physical, and psychological needs Under One Roof.





Purpose-built Infrastructure

The Senior Citizens Primary Care Unit prioritizes safety and security within its purpose-built infrastructure.
Handrails are strategically placed throughout the facility to provide stability and support for seniors as they move around.
Non-slip surfaces are implemented in areas prone to wet or slippery conditions, reducing the risk of falls.
Call buttons and alarms in washrooms ensure immediate aid in emergencies.
Regular maintenance upholds the highest safety standards, offering peace of mind to seniors and their families.
Our air-conditioned Gazebo Retreat, surrounded by green grounds, offers a serene environment for socializing over tea and snacks.
This space is designed to encourage relaxed conversations and further promote social bonds among seniors.

Basic Health Facilities

The SCPCU offers a range of basic health facilities to address the health concerns of seniors. This includes routine physical examinations, ensuring thorough care and necessary medical guidance for seniors' health needs. The unit also emphasized on specialized care for chronic conditions and focuses on disease management, monitoring, and necessary treatments..


Nutritional Services

We understand the critical role of proper nutrition in maintaining seniors' health. Our team has experienced dietitians who provide diet plans according to age, medical conditions, and dietary restrictions.
Diet plans are designed to provide balanced meals that incorporate essential nutrients, vitamins, and minerals that are necessary for healthy aging. We also provide nutritious meals and healthy snacks.

Executive Health
Check-up

With the busy life schedule and timelines to meet, we often tend to neglect our health and ignore little issues as insignificant threats to our health. At times, diseases are present with no symptoms and a diagnosis at a later stage may be too late to be treated. Having regular checkups can help detect any diseases at an early stage and give us a better chance of treatment National Hospital offers executives, entrepreneurs, business owners and any person who leads a busy schedule its hassle free service of “Executive Health Check-up”. Getting Executive Check-up done is a very valuable decision you can do for your health.

Department of Accident and Emergency

The Department of Accident and Emergency at LNH is one of the busiest emergency departments in the city of Karachi. We understand the critical nature of life-threatening medical conditions and traumas.We excel in rapid response services during any disaster in the city. The department is supported by on-call Physicians and Surgeons and the backup of the Critical Care Team. The service is provided 24 hours a day, 365 days per year.

Services

  • In House Radiology Unit
  • Pharmacy
  • Lab Counter
  • Emergency OPD
  • Casualty Operation Theatre (C.O.T)

Education and Training

Accident and Emergency department is recognized by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Pakistan to provide training for fellowship in Emergency Medicine. In addition, exposure and hands-on training to the trainees for fellowship in different specialties in acute management of critically ill patients is also provided.
